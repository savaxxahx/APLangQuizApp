Rhetorical Terms Test 2

Denotation

The strict, literal, dictionary definition of a word.

Diction

Refers to the writer's word choices, especially with regard to their correctness, clearness, or effectiveness.

Didactic

Works that have the primary aim of teaching or instructing, especially the teaching of morals or ethnic principles.

Euphemism

A more agreeable or less offensive substitute for a generally unpleasant word or concept.

Extended Metaphor

A metaphor developed at great length, occurrng frequently in or throughout the book.

Figurative Language

Writing or speech that is not intended to carry literal meaning and is usually meant to be imaginative and vivid.
